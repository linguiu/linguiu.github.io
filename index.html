TenTrix
Game

TenTrix is a captivating and addictive puzzle game that combines strategic thinking and spatial awareness. The goal is to place colorful blocks on a 10x10 grid to create and clear full lines, either horizontally or vertically. With no time limits, you can enjoy the challenge at your own pace, making it perfect for relaxing gameplay or sharpening your problem-solving skills. Plan your moves carefully to avoid running out of space and aim for a high score!

Merge Mine - Idle Clicker
Game

Merge Mine - Idle Clicker is an engaging and addictive game that blends the excitement of merging items with the relaxing mechanics of an idle clicker. Dig deep into the mine to discover valuable treasures, merge identical ores to unlock more powerful resources, and upgrade your mining tools for maximum efficiency. Whether you actively click to boost your progress or let your miners work in the background, the game offers endless fun and rewards. Perfect for players who enjoy casual gameplay with satisfying progression and discovery!

Body Drop 3D
Game

Body Drop 3D is a thrilling and physics-driven simulation game where you control a ragdoll character in a series of wild and chaotic scenarios. Test your creativity and strategic thinking as you drop, launch, and crash the ragdoll into various obstacles, aiming for high scores based on impact and destruction. With realistic physics, stunning 3D graphics, and endless opportunities for hilarious outcomes, Body Drop 3D delivers endless fun and excitement for players who love action-packed challenges!
